> **Written On:** 23-12-25 - **Last Updated:** 23-12-25

**0.3.3** is a minor release for *Stancements*, released on November 17, 2025.[^1] It adds recipes for making blue-colored items in the mod, as I had forgotten to add them previously.

## Additions
- Added recipes for blue crafting table cloth.
- Added recipes for blue concrete from a water bucket and concrete powder.
